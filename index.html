CatCollector!!

You will be given a random number at the beginnning of the game
There are four cats below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your local score.
You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your score goes above the random number
The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
