What's going on here??!!

You will be given a random number at the beginnning of the game. There are four cats below. By clicking on a cat you will add a specific amount of points to your local score. You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your score goes above the random number. The value of each cat is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal. In the end, however, with too many collected, nobody wins.
